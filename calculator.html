Carbon Emissions Calculator

Welcome to our carbon emissions calculator! This tool aims to estimate your average carbon emissions per year based on a series of questions. Before using this calculator, please note that it serves as an estimate of carbon emissions based on common daily habits. It is not intended to replace more comprehensive carbon footprint calculators, such as this carbon footprint calculator This calculator does not claim to be more accurate than existing tools.

Let's get started! Answer a few questions to gauge your carbon emissions.
